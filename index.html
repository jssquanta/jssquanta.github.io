About Us



Quanta,the official technical society of JSSATE,NOIDA aims at innovation and excellence. The organisation remains active around the year through workshops and events dealing in robotics.

It imparts knowledge to students through regular workshops related to mannual and automated robotics .The members are actively involved in organising events and keeping the trend.

From HOD's Desk

I am proud to see that the students of my department have put in substantial effort in running of the association. Department has been consistently producing highly knowledgeable and skill based graduates through quality education. By doing what you love reflects in the progress of any individual. If we truly love our job in hand and passionate about it, will be able to find a successful and progressive path very easily. Living everyday to the best of the individual ability, feeling content at the end of the day when you have given best at everything, irrespective of the result, keeps every individual, in high energy and happiness. I congratulate the team of faculty member and students for their brilliant and original efforts. Also I look forward to seeing the juniors taking up the responsibilities of this association in future, so that this tradition remains perpetual.

From Faculty's desk

Being the faculty coordinator of the association, I have seen it rising from the scratch to zenith this day. Quanta was developed with the motto to enhance the practical and theoretical knowledge of different domains of electronics and communication through regular workshops based on embedded, VLSI, simulation and robotics. I believe that this quest for excellence will continue.

Events This Year

Blitzkrieg

The premier line follower championship of the fest, BLITZKRIEG is the battle speed , wisdom, accuracy and precision. The task is to build a line follower which will have to traverse the path as fast as possible, passing through checkpoints, choosing the right lines to follow, negotiate sharp twists and turns. Experience the enthralling race against time, indulge in the technology and creativity, and witness these machines follow their paths- but only one lead to glory.

D'aguaBotica

The world is under attack. Doom and despair hung over our heads. The only way out is to build a Superbot , capable of traversing both land and water, clearing obstacles and performing various tasks! Do you have it what it takes to be a superhero , who can manoeuvre this superbot with utmost precision and handling ? Then come to our rescue in this Manual Robotics Event spanning across both land and water. Unleash the Superhero in you !

Starstixx

Being a superhero isn't about strenght only, but about being creative and constructive. Join us in this fun event which will bring out your creativity like never before! Think, solve and create in the shortest time and in the most innovative manner! Amazing prizes await!

Imitation Game

Are you far beyond the world….! Really….! Then come with us and enter the world of real challenges… Brush and twist your mind to cross through an amusing technical frisk and prove yourself to be the real super hero… Are you ready for the viel Spab…! Then welcome to our world.
